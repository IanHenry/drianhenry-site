About Dr Ian Henry

Connecting Data, Science & Leadership

Integrating analytical insight with human development

Overview

I’m a computational biologist, data science leader, and educator passionate about how data and people transform ideas into impact.
With over 15 years of experience spanning academia, biotech, and corporate R&D, I’ve worked at the intersection of science, analytics, and leadership — guiding individuals, teams, and organisations to grow through evidence, clarity, and collaboration.

I currently lead data science for Translational Medicine at AstraZeneca, applying data-driven insights to accelerate discovery and development in healthcare.
Alongside this, I mentor and coach scientists, professionals, and leaders through my independent tutoring, coaching, and consulting practice.

Experience Snapshot

Academic & Scientific Foundations

  • PhD in Genomics / Computational Biology – University of Nottingham
  • BSc in Molecular Biology – University of Edinburgh
  • Research at the Max Planck Institute for Molecular Cell Biology & Genetics
  • Published 25+ scientific papers in leading journals

Leadership & Transformation

  • MBA (Distinction) – Hult International Business School
  • CMgr FCMI – Chartered Management Institute (Senior Leaders Master’s, Distinction)
  • Led global analytics initiatives at AstraZeneca and European research institutions
  • Experienced in cross-functional project leadership and digital transformation

Mentoring, Coaching & Consulting

  • Tutoring and mentoring students from A-Level to postdoctoral level
  • Career and leadership coaching for scientists and professionals
  • Strategic consulting in data science, AI, and digital transformation
  • Proven record in combining technical and human development

Philosophy

My work is guided by three principles:

  1. Clarity through Data — turning complexity into insight and insight into impact.
  2. Growth through Learning — empowering individuals to understand, adapt, and lead.
  3. Connection through Collaboration — bridging disciplines, cultures, and perspectives to drive change.

Whether teaching, coaching, or consulting, I aim to bring structure, empathy, and evidence-based thinking to every interaction.
